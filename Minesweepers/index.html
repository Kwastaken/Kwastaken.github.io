How to play

  1. The goal is to uncover all squares without exploding a mine
  2. The amount of mines is shown by the flag
  3. Select the flag to mark potential mines(you have a limited amount)
  4. Select the shovel to uncover squares

      Good luck 😉


  Help color codes

  • Purple = dig with shovel
  • Green = place flag
  • Red = The number that is the cause of the action
  • Cyan = Unsure what to do so random
